Play Asteroids

Instructions

Welcome to asteroids captain! You need to naviagate the great beyond. Avoid collision with asteroids as they will destroy your ship! We only have 3 ships in total, so after that it is over for us. We can only move forward when all the asteroid is the current region have been destroyed. Once that happens we will move to the next region which will likely have more asteroids.

Controls:
  • W or UpArrow to thrust
  • A/D or LeftArrow/RightArrow to rotate ship
  • Space to fire laser
  • F to warp to a random location
